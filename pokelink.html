Welcome to my links page! Here I have listings of some cool web pages, a review, and a rating (you know, like PG-13 or stuff like that).

Here's a guide to the ratings:

"A"=All (suitable for everyone)
"7+"=Suitable for ages 7 and up
"T"=Teen (sutiable for ages 13 and up)
Now, for the links!

Pojo's Unofficial News & Price Guide Monthly Website
Address: http://www.pojo.com/pokemon.html
Rating: "7+"
Review: If you want complete info on all 251 Pokémon and up-to-date news and info, then pojo.com is the place for you! Their Pokédex is more complete than mine'll ever be, and their front page is filled with the latest developments! You'll have to check it out yourself to know the rest! Score: 5!

Nintendo's Official Pokémon World Website
Address: http://www.pokemon.com
Rating: "A"
Review: This is a neat little website. It has the latest info on new Pokémon video games and promotions, a Pokédex, cartoon reviews, and game walkthroughs, plus more! Score: 4+

Wizard of the Coast's Official Pokémon Trading Card Game Website
Address: http://www.wizards.com/pokemon
Rating: "7+"
Review: If you love the Pokémon TCG, then this is your website. Complete data on all English, French, German, Spanish, and Italian Pokémon cards. Score: 4-
