Pokémon The Movie 4--Celebi: A Timeless Encounter

Yes, folks, there will be a fourth Pokémon movie coming this summer! From the title and other reports (such as a movie trailer on pokemon2000.com), this movie obviously features Celebi, the time travel Pokémon. In the movie, Celebi falls to earth and a girl about Ash's age finds it. After escaping many different perils, such as an Ursaring attack, the minute Celebi obviously must find Ash & friends because it heals Misty's leg (?).
