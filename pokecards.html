Favorite TCG Cards

This page is images of some of my favorite Pokémon cards. Some of them are in here because I like their graphics. Some, because of their ability. And some for who knows what reason!

Anyway, here they are!

Neo 4 cards

I just got these over the Labor Day weekend. I can't read Japanese, but this looks like a good pack that I got; a Light Ninetales and Light Arcanine, plus 4 trainers. I wish I could read them....

Neo 2 Magnemite

I got this card recently in a trade. It may not be the best card in the world, but it is an improvement over its predecessors. It's also the first non-Lightning-type Magnemite.

Neo 2 Poliwrath

I got this card in my first Neo 2 booster. Unlike Magnemite, this card is plain awesome--30 for 2, and 70 for 4 with 20 to self? Git outta here! You could make a whole deck style after this guy!

Neo 2 Forretress

I also recently traded for this card--yet another Neo 2. This is an OK card in the Active spot--10 damage to your opponent's retreating Active, and a double-gust with 30 damage is pretty good. Could be good in the right deck.
